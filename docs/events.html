Upcoming Events

Check out what's going on with Virginia Polyamory! Everyone is welcome to the group and public events. There are no age restrictions unless specified in the event description.

New events are posted as soon as they are planned. Subscribe to the Google calendar, or visit the Facebook event page for more information. We look forward to seeing you!

Public Events

Poly 101

New to polyamory? Have questions? This is a great way to learn some basic information about polyamory and ethical non-monogamy.

Book Club

VA Polyamory hosts a monthly book club at a local library. We rotate between fiction and non-fiction, focussing on books with polyamorous and LGBTQ+ content. We can help you source a copy of the book, and there is never pressure to finish before the discussion (we'll try to minimize spoilers).

Brewery Social

We encourage everyone to bring their favorite card or tabletop game! This is a great opportunity to socialize directly with other polyamorous and poly-friendly folx.

VA Polyamory at Virginia Pride

Come find our booth at Richmond's Virginia PrideFest! Several of our volunteers are at Pridefest to share information about the concept of polyamory.
We also feature a Draw-Your-Polycule board! For more information about Virginia Pride, visit Diversity Richmond

Private Events

Poly Potluck

Bring your favorite dish and join other poly and poly-friendly folx for a Virginia Polyamory potluck! Children are welcome!

Please note that private events are only open to those that have attended a public event! Please contact us if you have any questions.
